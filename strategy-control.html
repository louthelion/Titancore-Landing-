Main Definition

Strategy & Control means choosing direction with discipline.

What does this page do?

This page helps visitors move from research to decision-making. After a person studies the market, customer, competitor, opportunity, risk, and information quality, they still need to decide how the business should position itself, what it should offer, how it should price, how it should grow, what it should avoid, and how decisions should be controlled.

The goal is to make business education useful. Visitors can click each strategy area and learn how to think before making major moves.
