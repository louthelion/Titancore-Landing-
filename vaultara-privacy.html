How Vaultara Capital handles information submitted through this website.

VC

Effective June 9, 2026.

Information you provide

Vaultara may collect contact details, service interests, goals, and other information you choose to submit through contact, intake, or feedback forms.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to requests, review potential service fit, provide private package information, improve website content, and maintain appropriate business records.

Sensitive information

Do not submit Social Security numbers, passwords, complete financial account numbers, or other highly sensitive credentials through general website forms.

Vaultara does not sell personal information submitted through these forms. Information may be handled by service providers that support website hosting, form processing, communications, or business operations, subject to their applicable terms and safeguards.

Your choices

You may contact Vaultara to ask about information you submitted or request a correction or deletion, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

Contact

Privacy questions may be sent through the Vaultara contact page.
