Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how TitanCore Holdings handles information submitted through public website forms, subscriptions, feedback, contact requests, and public communication channels.

Back Home ›

Last Updated

May 2026

Information We May Collect

TitanCore Holdings may collect information visitors choose to submit through website forms. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number, when provided
  • Subscription preferences
  • Feedback or message content
  • Selected interests, such as company news, global business news, market watch, or media updates

How Information May Be Used

TitanCore Holdings may use submitted information to:

  • Respond to public inquiries
  • Send requested updates or media briefings
  • Review feedback and suggestions
  • Improve public website pages, articles, media content, and visitor experience
  • Organize public communication connected to TitanCore Holdings and its company network

Forms, Subscriptions, and Feedback

When visitors submit a subscription, feedback message, or contact request, the information may be received through website form services and sent to TitanCore Holdings for review. Feedback is reviewed privately first and is not automatically published publicly.

Third-Party Platforms

TitanCore Holdings may provide links to official social media pages such as Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn. When visitors leave the TitanCore website and visit those platforms, the privacy practices of those platforms may apply.

Cookies and Analytics

TitanCore Holdings may later use website tools, analytics, or hosting services that help understand website traffic, visitor activity, form submissions, and page performance. If additional tracking tools are added in the future, this policy may be updated.

Information Sharing

TitanCore Holdings does not intend to sell visitor information. Information may be used internally to manage public communications, subscriptions, feedback, website inquiries, and business-related public contact.

Data Security

TitanCore Holdings aims to keep submitted information handled responsibly. However, no website or online transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. Visitors should avoid submitting sensitive private information through public website forms.

Children’s Privacy

The TitanCore Holdings public website is intended for general business and public information purposes. It is not designed to collect information from children.

Policy Updates

TitanCore Holdings may update this Privacy Policy as the website, forms, media features, subscriptions, and public communication systems develop.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact TitanCore Holdings at contact@titancoreholdings.com or through the official contact page.

Contact us today

For general questions, privacy-related questions, or professional inquiries, contact TitanCore Holdings through the official contact page.
