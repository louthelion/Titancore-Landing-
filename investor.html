Capital Direction

TitanCore Decides the Use of Capital

No company picking

This structure protects the full TitanCore ecosystem. Investors do not choose only Family First, Vaultara, VitalPath, or RevMotion. They submit interest to TitanCore Holdings, and TitanCore decides where capital may be needed and how the structure should be reviewed.

1. Investor Applies to TitanCore

The investor submits interest to TitanCore Holdings only.

2. TitanCore Reviews Qualification

TitanCore reviews the request, investor type, message, and possible fit.

3. TitanCore Determines Structure

TitanCore decides whether repayment, interest, profit-share, equity, or another structure should be discussed.

4. Written Agreement Required

No payment, return, or acceptance exists unless TitanCore approves and a written agreement is completed.

Example only: if TitanCore later approves an investor, TitanCore may discuss how money could be returned, whether by repayment, interest, profit-sharing, equity, or another structure. The public website does not promise weekly payments, monthly payments, interest, profits, acceptance, or returns.
