Global News Purpose

International news belongs on its own page.

Global News should be separate from the main News & Media page. News & Media can cover TitanCore updates, media information, U.S. business news, and state-level context, while Global News focuses on international movement.

This structure keeps the website clean, organized, and professional. Visitors who want worldwide context can come to this page without crowding the homepage or the general media hub.

Country-Level Focus

Global News can later include country-level summaries, international business updates, and worldwide market context without going too deep into city-level news.
