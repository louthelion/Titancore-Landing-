Why it matters

Market Watch helps visitors read the business environment before major decisions.

  • Borrowing costs can change what a business can afford.
  • Energy movement can change delivery, utility, and production costs.
  • Real estate trends can affect rent, collateral, and property strategy.
  • Technology and AI movement can change productivity and competition.
  • Market confidence can affect customer behavior and expansion timing.
