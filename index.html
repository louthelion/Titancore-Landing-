Dashboard Structure

This public site is being rebuilt with TitanCore Dashboard and Central Dashboard in mind from the beginning.

TitanCore Dashboard

TitanCore Dashboard is the company-level control room. Public TitanCore submissions should land into TitanCore intake records first, where internal review, routing, status tracking, and later AI action will begin.

Central Dashboard

Central Dashboard remains the top-level oversight layer. It is not forgotten. TitanCore Dashboard will later feed selected activity, summaries, and reporting upward into Central for leadership visibility and cross-company control.
