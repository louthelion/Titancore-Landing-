Live Connection Plan

This page needs APIs to become fully live.

The page design is the first step. To show live stock prices, crypto prices, commodities, forex, global markets, and alerts, TitanCore will need a data connection behind the page.

Best future setup

  • Market Data API: brings live prices, daily changes, and market movement.
  • News API: brings business news and major announcements.
  • OpenAI / AI Summary: turns market movement into simple public language.
  • Supabase: stores daily briefings, alerts, snapshots, and archives.
  • Netlify Functions or Replit Backend: protects API keys and connects the data safely.
  • Dashboard Review: later allows private review and approval of public updates.

This is why the static page can look complete now, but the real automatic daily updates come later when the API and backend are connected.
