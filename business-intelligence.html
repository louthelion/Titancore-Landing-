How Signals Move Through Business

  • Rates up — credit tightens and refinancing costs rise.
  • Inflation up — input costs rise and pricing pressure increases.
  • Energy up — transportation and operating costs increase.
  • Liquidity down — lenders become more selective.
  • Confidence up — hiring and expansion can improve.
