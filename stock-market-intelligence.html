How To Read The Market

Market movement should be reviewed with context.

A stock going down does not always mean a company is weak. A stock going up does not always mean a company is safe. Visitors should review the reason behind the movement.

Questions visitors should ask

  • Did the price move because of company news?
  • Did the price move because of the whole market?
  • Did interest rates affect the movement?
  • Did inflation, jobs, housing, or banking news affect the movement?
  • Is the movement short-term or part of a longer trend?
  • Is there real business strength behind the movement?
  • Is the market reacting from fear, confidence, or uncertainty?
  • What information should be verified before making a decision?
