Learn Before You Apply

Payment History

Paying on time is one of the most important credit habits. Late payments may hurt a credit profile and make approval harder.

Credit Utilization

Using too much of a credit card limit may hurt the score. Many people try to keep balances low compared with available credit.

Business Bank Account

Separating personal and business money can help with organization, accounting, and future financial review.

Funding Readiness

Lenders may review revenue, time in business, credit, bank statements, tax records, and business stability.
