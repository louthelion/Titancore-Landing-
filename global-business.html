Global Business Meaning

Why Global Movement Reaches Local Companies.

Global shipping affects prices because port delays, freight costs, and route changes can move inventory timing and import expenses. Currency movement affects imports and exports by changing purchasing power and contract costs. Oil and energy affect transportation, utilities, delivery, and production.

Real estate and infrastructure can show growth, investment, population movement, and commercial opportunity. Global banking affects capital movement, credit availability, and investor confidence. International markets can affect U.S. businesses through suppliers, customers, rates, financing, and confidence.
